About me

My name is Cheddi Charles. I am the oldest of five brothers. I was born from the beautiful island of Grenada located south of the Caribbean sea. Growing up was not easy because we have to work hard to get what you want.

Sports is one of my favorite hobbies. I play cricket, soccer and basketball whenever I get the chance. I always dream of playing for my country but I was not good enough. At a young age, I had a liking for numbers and maths becasue i was never really at English and writing even up today. I used my math skills to help me solve problems and went on to do my first degree in mathematics.

Currently I am a high school math teacher. Outside of work , I like going out to the movies, road trips, and out door activities.
